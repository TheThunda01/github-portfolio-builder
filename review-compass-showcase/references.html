References and rationale

Every major guidance point is tied to a source.

Review Compass is an educational planning tool. It summarizes reliable methods guidance in plain language, but students should still consult the original sources, mentors, and librarians before using a plan for a formal protocol or manuscript.

Used as the main backbone for systematic-review planning, including scope, questions, eligibility, searching, data collection, risk of bias, synthesis, and GRADE.
